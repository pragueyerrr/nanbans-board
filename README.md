# NanBan's Board

A personal job tracker built for my friend Nandita (we call her NanBan), a product management professional looking for PM and adjacent roles in Dubai.

---

## What is this?

It's a job board that scrapes Dubai-based product management roles and presents them in one focused place. PM jobs are scattered all over the internet — this pulls them together, filters for the roles that actually make sense for someone in Nandita's field, and keeps things fresh.

Nandita can also add any job she finds elsewhere: paste a URL and the app tries to autofill the details, or fill it in manually. Whether it's something scraped by the board or a role she spotted herself, it all lives in one place.

## What does it do?

- **Scrapes fresh jobs** from multiple sources — Adzuna, Indeed, JSearch, and direct employer sites (Careem, Noon, Tabby, Tamara, Jumeirah, and more) — filtered for PM and adjacent roles in Dubai
- **AI match scoring** — upload your CV and the app scores how well each job fits you, with a breakdown of what aligns and what doesn't
- **AI resume generation** — one click generates a tailored resume for a specific job, downloadable as PDF or Word (.doc)
- **AI cover letter** — writes a personalised cover letter for each role, streamed in real time, downloadable as PDF or Word (.doc)
- **Save jobs** — bookmark anything worth tracking so it shows up in your Applications tab
- **Add your own jobs** — paste a URL from anywhere and the app autofills what it can, or add details manually

## Who is it for?

Nandita, specifically. But if you're a PM job hunting in Dubai, it'll work just as well for you.
